# Seminar on key experiments of the Aharonov-Bohm-Effect

## Content

This repository contains my seminar handout and original figures on major experiments related to the Aharonov-Bohm effect. The material was created for my contribution to the "Key Experiments in Quantum Science" seminar at Heidelberg University.

The handout focuses in particular on Chambers' 1960 experiment and Tonomura's 1985 experiment. I created several new figures to explain the main experimental ideas and physical concepts in a compact and accessible way.

## Citation and Reuse

The figures and handout are free to use. If you use them in your own work, teaching material, presentation, or notes, please cite this repository or link to it.
